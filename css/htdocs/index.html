Hello and welcome to my website!!! This website will just give you the fundamentals you need in order to start one of my favorite franchises, Fire Emblem.
Fire Emblem is a popular turn-based tactical RPG video franchise developed by Intelligent Systems, and published by Nintendo over many platforms.
A mechanic that separates Fire Emblem from most tactical RPGs is 'permadeath', which means that when a character dies, they don't revive, forcing the player to be careful.
This website of mine will act as a little 'guide' of sorts for new players that are willing to tackle the franchise in its entirety, which would include where to begin, and where to go from there.
